Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Shipping can sometimes be confusing. That’s why we’ve compiled a list of frequently asked questions to help you get the information you need quickly. If you don’t find what you’re looking for, feel free to contact our support team!

What shipping options do you offer?

We offer standard, express, and international shipping options to suit your delivery needs

How can I track my shipment?

You can track your shipment in real-time using our tracking tool. Simply enter your tracking number on our website to get the latest updates.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times depend on the destination and shipping method. Standard shipping takes 3-7 days, while express shipping can take as little as 1-2 days.

What happens if my package is delayed?

If your package is delayed, our system will notify you with updates. You can also contact our support team for further assistance.

Do you offer refunds for lost or damaged packages?

Yes, we have a refund and compensation policy for lost or damaged shipments. Please review our terms or contact support for assistance.
